Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2026

Welcome to Science 6-Marker Hub (accessible from science6markerhub.com). Your privacy is incredibly important to us. This policy outlines the types of data collected when you use our revision tools and how that information is handled.

1. Information We Collect

Science 6-Marker Hub is a free educational resource. We do not require you to create an account, register, or provide personal identity details like your name or phone number to access our revision material. However, we do use third-party tools that automatically log basic analytical metrics.

2. Google AdSense & Third-Party Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website to support our operations. Google, as a third-party vendor, utilizes tracking cookies to serve relevant ads to our visitors based on their browsing behavior on our site and other locations across the Internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies allows it and its network partners to show ads targeted directly to your general interests.
  • You can choose to opt out of personalized tracking advertisements at any time by adjusting your browser cookie profiles or by visiting the official Google Ads Settings page.

3. Analytical Tracking Tools

We use Umami Analytics to monitor our general user activity, page performance, and overall volume counts (such as tracking how many students view our physics, chemistry, or biology sections). This collection is completely anonymized and does not log individual identifiers or personal tracking data.

4. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you have rights regarding your web data under international regulations (including GDPR and CCPA). You have the right to request access to, update, or ask for the deletion of any collected cookies. You can manage these permissions directly via your local browser preferences.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or require further clarification regarding this Privacy Policy page, you can reach out directly via our GitHub project portal.
